Our Comprehensive Services

From strategy to execution, we cover every aspect of your digital marketing journey.

Consulting & Advisory

Leverage our deep expertise in Data & AI strategy, MarTech rearchitecture, and experience reinvention to define your digital roadmap. We provide actionable insights and strategic guidance to unlock new growth opportunities.

  • Data & AI Strategy Development
  • MarTech Stack Audit & Optimization
  • Customer Experience (CX) Design
  • Digital Transformation Roadmapping

Enablement Solutions

Build robust foundations for your digital success. We specialize in seamless technology integrations, developing cutting-edge digital, mobile, and web3 applications, and establishing efficient Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs).

  • Marketing Cloud Integration (Adobe, Salesforce, etc.)
  • Custom Digital & Mobile App Development
  • Web3 Strategy & Blockchain Integration
  • Workflow Automation & SOP Implementation

Operations & Augmentation

Enhance your internal capabilities by augmenting your teams with our specialized digital marketing experts. We provide scalable operational support to drive efficiency and achieve your marketing goals without the overhead.

  • SEO & Content Marketing Operations
  • Paid Media (PPC, Social Ads) Management
  • Email Marketing & CRM Execution
  • Analytics & Reporting Support

Managed Services

For a truly hands-off approach, our managed services offer end-to-end planning, architecture, enablement, and daily operations for all your digital marketing priorities. We act as an extension of your team, dedicated to your growth.

  • Full-Funnel Digital Campaign Management
  • Customer Journey Optimization
  • Performance Marketing & ROI Tracking
  • Comprehensive Digital Strategy Execution

About Xerago

Driving intelligence-driven digital marketing for global brands since the early 2000s.

Our Journey & Mission

Founded in the early 2000s, Xerago emerged with a vision to revolutionize digital marketing through data and intelligence. From our headquarters in Chennai, India, we've grown to establish a global presence with offices in the United States, UAE, and Singapore.

Our mission is to empower mid-market and enterprise brands across five continents to achieve their acquisition and conversion goals. We do this by transforming complex data into decisive, actionable strategies that drive measurable growth and unparalleled customer experiences.
